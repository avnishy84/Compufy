# Product

Compufy Technology is a corporate marketing website for a technology company. It presents the company's brand, services, and contact information with a modern dark-mode UI.

## Pages
- **Home** - Landing page with hero section and "what we do" overview
- **Services** - Categorized service offerings with service cards
- **Contact** - Contact form with submission handling and success state
- **Coming Soon** - Placeholder for unreleased sections

## Key Characteristics
- Dark-mode-first design with glassmorphism accents
- SSR-enabled for fast initial page loads
- Firebase backend for form submissions
- Responsive, single-page feel with lazy-loaded routes
